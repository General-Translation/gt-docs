# Which AI models do you use?

<Callout type="info">This information is accurate as of August 15th, 2024. It may not be updated in real-time.</Callout>

We use a combination of the latest models from [Anthropic](https://anthropic.com) and [OpenAI](https://openai.com), depending on the specifics of your translation request.

## Free plan

We may use `gpt-4o-mini` or `claude-3-haiku-20240307`, or a combination of those models, to translate your request.

## Paid plans

We may use `gpt-4o`, `gpt-4o-mini`, `claude-3-5-sonnet-20240620`, or `claude-3-haiku-20240307`, or a combination of those models, to translate your request.
